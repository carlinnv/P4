My Blog Posts

Trip to the BBG

I went on a trip to the Brooklyn Botanic Garden to see the cherry blossoms! We went in mid-April, when many of the blossoms are hitting peak bloom. The Japanese garden was beautiful this time of year; I highly recommend booking a ticket to see the garden.

Upstate New York

Over spring break, my friends and I booked an AirBNB in upstate New York for a little getaway. The peace and quiet of the area, which was surrounded by mountains and lakes, provided a calming break from our hectic school schedules.

Meeting at the MET

For the second time this school year, I went to the MET for an English assignment. To be more accurate, my friends had an English assignment and I went along for the ride. There were a ton of cool new exhibits that I definitely recommend checking out.

Catching up on the ice

Over winter break, I caught up with some of my high school friends at a skating rink near our house. Since there was literally nobody there, we got to skate past our alloted time slot for about two hours, for only $15!
