Inspiration of The Month

My current inspiration is an artist who many of you fellow art hobbyists might know: Kim Jung Gi. Kim Jung Gi was a South Korean illustrator who inspired many with his exuberant art style and ability to make illustrated scenes come to life. He is known for his mastery over perspective and details.

Other Inspirations

Rembert Montald

Montald is an artist at Riot Games. I discovered him on the Youtuber Proko's channel, where he did a tour of his very impressive notebook and demonstrated his ability to draw from life.

J.C. Leyendecker

Leyendecker is well known for his knowledge on clothing folds, as you can see by his realistic interpretations of them, but I also appreciate the pop of color he adds to his art pieces.

Posuka Demizu

Posuka Demizu was the primary artist on The Promised Neverland. Outside of the show, however, she is also fantastic at using dynamic angles and portraying scenes full of life.

Emily Xu

I discovered Emily Xu through her Youtube animatics. I love the way she incorporates different textures and colors into her paintings.
